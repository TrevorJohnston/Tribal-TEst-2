Welcome to Tribal Surf Style

Tribal Surf began as a personal hobby to create authentic leather and pearl jewelry at a reasonable price for myself and others. As a one-person operation, every piece is crafted by hand, using freshwater pearls and authentic leather to create beautiful and durable pieces. By being surrounded by the beauty of the coast and the inspiring people in my life has allowed me to hone in on my creativity and blend natural materials with timeless styles.

Our Story

Each piece I create embraces the natural beauty of cultured pearls, chosen not for their high end appeal but rather for their genuine charm and character. This approach allows me to craft handmade jewelry that reflects both my creativity and commitment.
